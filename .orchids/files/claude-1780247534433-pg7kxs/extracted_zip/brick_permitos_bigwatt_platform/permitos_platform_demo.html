Brick PermitOS for BigWatt Digital

AI platform for air + water permits, CFR document automation, PTE/NPDES modeling, digital-twin simulation, and continuous compliance controls.

What this platform does

Brick PermitOS is a single AI workflow that can replace most manual permitting workstreams: site intake, applicability screening, PTE, CFR matrices, BACT/LAER, AERMOD inputs, NPDES/SWPPP/SPCC/316(b), and operational compliance. Unlike a traditional consultant, it also connects the permit to actual control logic: turbine runtime, cooling load, water use, blowdown, battery dispatch, and compliance logs.
